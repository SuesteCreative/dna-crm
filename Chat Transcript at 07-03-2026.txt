[14:43:53] Peony: Thanks for the details.
Since Staff 1 has a busy event from 11:30 to 12:00, the slot 11:20–11:50 overlaps with that event, so that staff is not available for that slot.
For the next slot 12:00–12:20, the busy event ends exactly at 12:00, which is the start of the slot. In this case, the system may still treat that staff as unavailable to avoid bookings immediately adjacent to another event.
So for that time slot the system detects:
* Staff 2 > available
* Staff 3 > available
* Staff 1 > unavailable
That’s why the widget shows 2 available spots.
